Privacy Policy

DiceKeep • Last updated: July 2, 2026

DiceKeep respects your privacy.

Data collection

DiceKeep does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal user data.

Local storage

Character data, notes, portraits, inventory, spells, and other app content are stored locally on your device.

Photos and images

If you choose a portrait for a character, the selected image is used only inside the app and remains on your device.

Internet and accounts

DiceKeep does not require an account and does not use online synchronization.

Permissions

DiceKeep may request access to photos or media only when you choose a portrait for a character. Selected images remain on your device and are never uploaded.

Third-party services

DiceKeep does not use advertising networks, analytics services, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Children's privacy

DiceKeep does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.

Contact
